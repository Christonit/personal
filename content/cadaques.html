Cadaqués Bayahibe

  • User Experience & Website design

Cadaqués Bayahibe is a hotel recreation of a European village bathed in a waterfront of a white sandy beach and crystal clear turquoise water, located in Bayahibe, considered one of the most spectacular beaches in Dominican Republic.

VISIT WEBSITE

Task

In 2015 Working for NEARVANA, I was tasked with creating a new website that follows the new graphics guidelines, reflecting at the same time all that makes this resort such a wonderful place.

Solution

I crafted wireframes and mockups, when they were approved I searched for a wordpress template that most closely followed our vision and heavely customized it.
